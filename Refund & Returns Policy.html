1. Overview

At GlobalTechCore, we stand behind the quality of our products and services. This Refund and Returns Policy explains your rights and our obligations regarding refunds, exchanges, and cancellations. It applies to all purchases made through our website, including exam vouchers, dumps, training courses, and bundles.

We strive to be fair and transparent. Our policy reflects the unique nature of digital goods and third-party vouchers while prioritizing customer satisfaction.

💰 30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee:

Most physical and unused digital products are eligible for a refund within 30 days of purchase. See specific product categories for details.

2. Exam Vouchers

2.1 Unused, Unregistered Vouchers

Exam vouchers that have not been used, registered, or scheduled with the certification vendor are eligible for a refund within 30 days of purchase. A $15 processing fee will be deducted from the refund amount to cover administrative and vendor restocking costs.

2.2 Used, Registered, or Expired Vouchers

Not eligible for refund. Once a voucher code has been revealed, registered to a candidate, or used to schedule an exam, it cannot be returned. Expired vouchers cannot be refunded or extended by GlobalTechCore; however, some vendors offer paid extensions directly.

2.3 Voucher Extensions

We cannot extend voucher expiration dates. Please contact the certification vendor directly for extension options (fees may apply).

4. Training & Bootcamps

4.1 Live, Instructor-Led Training

Cancellations and refunds for live bootcamps are based on the time remaining before the course start date:

  • 14+ days before start: 100% refund (minus $25 admin fee)
  • 7–13 days before start: 50% refund
  • 3–6 days before start: 25% refund
  • Less than 48 hours or after start: No refund; credit toward future course (valid 12 months)

4.2 Self-Paced Training

Self-paced courses (pre-recorded videos, labs) are non-refundable once access has been granted. If you have not logged in or accessed any content within 14 days of purchase, you may request a refund (subject to 15% restocking fee).

4.3 Course Transfers

You may transfer your registration to another batch or to another person once, at no cost, up to 7 days before the start date. Transfers requested within 7 days incur a $50 fee.

5. Bundles & Packages

Bundles (e.g., Voucher + Dumps, Training + Voucher + Job Support) are eligible for refunds as follows:

  • If no component has been used/downloaded: Full refund (minus $25 admin fee) within 30 days.
  • If one or more digital components have been downloaded: Refund is prorated based on the retail value of the unused components, minus admin fees. The used components are non-refundable.
  • If training has started or voucher registered: Only unused components may be refunded (e.g., job support).

Example: Security+ Ultimate Bundle ($399) – Voucher ($235) + Dumps ($39) + Job Support ($149). If dumps downloaded, refund = $235 (voucher) + $149 (job support) - $25 admin = $359.
